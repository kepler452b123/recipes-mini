Ceasar Salad Recipe

Description

Savor the essence of simplicity with our Caesar Salad, a culinary ode to uncomplicated perfection. Crisp romaine lettuce leaves form the base, offering a refreshing and satisfying crunch. A light drizzle of creamy Caesar dressing gently coats the leaves, infusing them with a classic, tangy flavor. Parmesan cheese shavings add a subtle richness, complementing the freshness of the greens. The simplicity of this salad allows the natural textures and tastes to take center stage, making each bite a harmonious blend of crispness, creaminess, and a hint of savory delight.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Croutons
  • Ceasar Sauce

Instructions

  1. Put romaine lettuce into a bowl
  2. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top of the lettuce
  3. Add ceasar sauce
  4. Add croutons
