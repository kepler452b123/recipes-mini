Pizza Recipe

Description

Experience the simplicity of a classic Cheese Pizza, where the harmony of flavors lies in the basics. A golden-brown crust serves as the canvas for a generous layer of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, creating a gooey and satisfying texture. The mild, creamy notes of the cheeses intertwine with the savory essence of tomato sauce, providing a timeless and comforting flavor profile. Each bite offers a delightful balance of crispness from the crust and the smooth, melty allure of the cheese. This Cheese Pizza is a testament to the beauty found in simplicity, allowing the fundamental elements to shine through.

Ingredients

  • Bread Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Instructions

  1. Knead bread dough and form into a circular shape
  2. Layer the bread with tomato sauce or paste
  3. Add a layer (or two) of cheese
  4. Put in the oven for 10-20 minutes at 400+ degrees Faranheit
