Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Delight in the exquisite simplicity of Butter Chicken, a culinary masterpiece where flavors meld effortlessly. Tender pieces of chicken are bathed in a rich, velvety tomato-based curry sauce, infused with a medley of aromatic spices. The creaminess of butter adds a luscious, smooth texture, enhancing the overall indulgence. Each bite offers a harmonious blend of succulent chicken and the nuanced warmth of spices, creating a symphony of taste that's both comforting and satisfying. The simplicity of this dish allows the essence of the ingredients to shine, making Butter Chicken a timeless and flavorful experience.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, diced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  1. In a large skillet, melt half of the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining butter. Saut√© onions until golden brown.
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in garam masala, turmeric powder, ground cumin, and chili powder. Cook for another 2 minutes until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the tomato puree and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked chicken back into the skillet, ensuring it's well-coated with the sauce.
  8. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring gently to combine. Simmer for an additional 5-7 minutes.
  9. Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
